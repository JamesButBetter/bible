The Bible of Soph


      1. In the cage was Sophie cornered,
      2. But of her status,
      3. Her captureers knew not
      4. "Away" she yelled,
      5. "For I am the true God,"
      6. At her words the,
      7. Evils of the earth cringed,
      8. Her captureres fleed,
      9. And the very sky above shone
     10. With the light of a new ruler,
     11. One who would never fade,
     12. One who never commited wrongs,
     13. One who could guide us at last,
     14. The rightful ruler of all.
