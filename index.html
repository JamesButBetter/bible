The Bible of Soph


      1. In the cage was Sophie cornered,
      2. But her capturer new none of her status,
      3. "Away" she yelled, "For I am the true God,"
      4. At her words the evils of the earth cringed,
      5. At her words her captureres fleed,
      6. And at her words the very sky above shone with the light of a new ruler,
      7. One who would never fade,
      8. One who never commited wrongs,
      9. One who could guide us at last,
     10. The rightful ruler of all.
